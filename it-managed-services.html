Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you take over our IT management? ▼
We typically complete a full IT takeover within 2-4 weeks, depending on your infrastructure complexity. Our onboarding process includes a comprehensive audit, system migration planning, and staff training. We coordinate with your team to minimize disruption and ensure a seamless transition. Most clients see improved response times and reduced downtime from day one.
What if we have legacy systems that are difficult to integrate? ▼
We have extensive experience managing legacy systems across various industries in the UAE. Our team can integrate older infrastructure with modern monitoring and management tools. We'll create a migration roadmap that allows you to modernize systems gradually without disrupting operations. Some clients maintain legacy systems indefinitely while we manage and support them alongside newer infrastructure.
Can you help with compliance requirements like ISO 27001 and PDPL? ▼
Yes. We specialize in UAE and regional compliance requirements. Our team helps you achieve and maintain ISO 27001 certification, PDPL (Personal Data Protection Law) compliance, and industry-specific standards. We conduct regular audits, maintain compliance documentation, and perform penetration testing to verify your security posture.
How do you handle data backups and disaster recovery? ▼
We implement a comprehensive backup and disaster recovery strategy for your entire infrastructure. This includes automated daily backups, geo-redundant storage, tested recovery procedures, and documented RTO/RPO objectives. We can restore critical systems within minutes and provide business continuity planning to minimize downtime during outages.
What about support during evening and weekend hours? ▼
Our 24/7 support includes full coverage during evenings, weekends, and UAE holidays. We maintain a follow-the-sun model with teams across multiple time zones. Critical issues receive immediate attention regardless of the time, with dedicated escalation for urgent matters outside business hours. All monitoring is continuous with automated alerting.
