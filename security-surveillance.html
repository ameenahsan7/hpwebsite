Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common security system questions

What is the difference between analog and IP cameras?

IP cameras offer superior image quality, remote viewing capabilities, and integration with modern systems. They transmit video over network infrastructure, allowing real-time monitoring from anywhere. Analog cameras are more traditional and cost-effective but lack advanced features.

How much storage do I need for CCTV footage?

Storage requirements depend on camera resolution, frame rate, and retention period. A 4MP camera recording 24/7 needs approximately 500GB-1TB per month. We recommend sizing systems for 30-90 days of storage based on your security needs and budget.

Is biometric access control secure?

Biometric systems are highly secure as they use unique biological characteristics that cannot be duplicated or forgotten like passwords. Fingerprint, facial recognition, and iris scanning systems provide excellent security for critical areas. We implement enterprise-grade solutions with encryption and backup authentication methods.

Can I integrate security systems with my existing infrastructure?

Yes, modern security systems are designed for integration. We work with your existing network infrastructure, smart building systems, and IT setup. Our solutions support integration with building automation, access control, CCTV, and analytics platforms for seamless operation.

What support and maintenance is included?

We provide comprehensive support including system design, professional installation, training, and ongoing maintenance. Our managed security services include monitoring, regular inspections, software updates, and 24/7 technical support to ensure your systems operate at peak performance.
