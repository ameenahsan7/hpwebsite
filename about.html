Our Story

Founded in Dubai over a decade ago, Anodic has grown from a specialized ELV (Extra Low Voltage) installation company into a full-service technology partner. Today, we're headquartered in Business Bay and serve hundreds of clients across the UAE.

With a commitment to excellence and innovation, we've completed 150+ projects spanning smart building automation, IT infrastructure, managed services, security systems, and more. Our journey is built on trust, expertise, and a genuine passion for solving complex technology challenges.
