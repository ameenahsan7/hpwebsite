Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our IT infrastructure services

What is Cat6A cabling and why do I need it?

Cat6A cabling supports speeds up to 10 Gbps and offers better shielding than Cat6, making it ideal for future-proofing your network infrastructure. It's essential for enterprises requiring high bandwidth, low latency, and reliable connections for mission-critical applications.

How does network security prevent cyber threats?

Our multi-layered security approach includes firewalls, IDS/IPS systems, network segmentation, and zero-trust architecture. These technologies work together to detect, prevent, and respond to threats in real-time, protecting your critical business data and systems.

What are the benefits of enterprise Wi-Fi over standard Wi-Fi?

Enterprise Wi-Fi offers superior performance, reliability, security, and coverage. It supports thousands of concurrent users, includes advanced management features, provides seamless roaming, and delivers consistent speeds across your facility—essential for modern workplaces.

How can VPN improve remote work security?

VPN creates encrypted tunnels for data transmission, hiding user location and protecting sensitive information from interception. Combined with multi-factor authentication, it ensures only authorized users can access your network resources securely from any location.

What maintenance does structured cabling require?

Quality structured cabling requires minimal maintenance—periodic visual inspections, documentation updates, and testing during network changes. Proper installation with good cable management and organization significantly extends the lifespan of your cabling infrastructure.
